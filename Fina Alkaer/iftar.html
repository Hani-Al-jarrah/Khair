Iftar Ramadan

Mawa’ed Al-Rahman

is a charitable initiative held during the holy month of Ramadan,

where free Iftar meals are provided to fasting individuals, especially those in need and travelers. The program can be organized by charities, mosques, or individuals who wish to spread kindness and generosity during this blessed month. Our initiative includes contributions from Tkiyet Um Ali and the Crown Prince Foundation, supporting our mission to provide meals and assistance to those in need, ensuring that everyone can experience the spirit of Ramadan with dignity and compassion.

Iftar for Orphans

is a charitable initiative held during the holy month of Ramadan,

where special Iftar meals are provided to orphaned children, offering them a warm and caring environment to break their fast. The program can be organized by charities, mosques, or individuals who wish to spread kindness and generosity during this blessed month. Our initiative includes contributions from Tkiyet Um Ali, Yarmouk University and the Crown Prince Foundation, supporting our mission to bring joy and togetherness to orphans, ensuring they experience the spirit of Ramadan with love, dignity, and compassion.
